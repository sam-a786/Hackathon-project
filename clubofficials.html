Club Officials

Here are our esteemed club officials

    Ian Cooper

    “Hi, I’m Ian Cooper Head and Co-Founder of Extreme Ironing Association. I’m also a 3x gold medallist of the Extreme Ironing Olympics for 3 consecutive years. I’ve been extreme ironing since the early 00’s started off in my childhood treehouse and then to my greatest achievement – the top of Ben Nevis.”


    Alexia Zoumpoulaki

    “Hi, I’m Alexia Zoumpoulaki Co-Founder of Extreme Ironing Association. I was the first women to win a gold medal at the Extreme Ironing Olympics ’09 and 2x silver medallist 2010. I’ve successfully extreme ironed in over 7 countries and 3 national landscapes including the leaning tower of Pisa.”


    Helene Di Ribaupierre

    “Hi, I’m Helene De Ribaupierre Head Coach of Extreme Ironing Association. I was the first person to Iron a T-shirt underwater at a depth of 42 m (138 ft) in the world's deepest pool (Y-40) in Montegrotto Terme Italy.
